Our Story

Cleaning Elevated to an Art Form

Founded in Orlando in 2014, Lumière Clean was born from a simple belief: your environment shapes your life. A cleaner space means a clearer mind, more joy, and better living.

What started as a two-person residential team has grown into Orlando's most trusted cleaning service, with over 50 trained specialists, cutting-edge equipment, and a commitment to eco-friendly practices that protect your family and the planet.

Every member of our team is background-checked, fully insured, and trained to our gold-standard protocol. We don't just clean — we restore, refresh, and renew.
